Common Questions About Cars

What is the best car for fuel efficiency?
Cars like the Toyota Prius, Honda Insight, and Hyundai Ioniq are known for their excellent fuel efficiency. Hybrid and electric cars typically offer the best mileage.
Should I buy an electric car or a petrol car?
Electric cars are great for reducing environmental impact and long-term savings. However, petrol cars may offer more range and are better for remote areas with fewer charging stations.
What documents do I need to buy a car?
You typically need a valid ID, proof of income, proof of address, insurance details, and a driver's license. Requirements may vary by region.

How often should I service my car?
Most manufacturers recommend a service every 6 months or 10,000 km, whichever comes first. Check your owner's manual for specifics.
