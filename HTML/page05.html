Car Categories

SUV

Spacious and versatile. Great for families and off-road driving. Popular models: Toyota Highlander, Ford Explorer.

Sedan

Comfortable and fuel-efficient. Ideal for daily commuting. Examples: Honda Accord, Toyota Camry.

Sports Car

Built for speed and agility. Sleek design. Examples: Porsche 911, Ford Mustang.

Supercar

High performance and luxury. Meant for enthusiasts. Examples: Lamborghini Revuelto, Ferrari SF90.

Hatchback

Compact and city-friendly. Affordable and efficient. Examples: Suzuki Swift, Mini Cooper.

Electric

Zero-emission, quiet, and high-tech. Top models: Tesla Model 3, MG ZS EV.
